Welcome to Our Good Neighbour, the volunteer event platform that helps you find and participate in volunteer events as an individual or group.

With Our Good Neighbour, you can:

  • Browse our calendar of indoor and outdoor events, filtered by category, location, and date to find the perfect one for you.
  • Join food drives, voter registration drives, tutoring events, and more.
  • Organize your own volunteer events and connect with others to build community and create positive change through service.

We're so excited to have you join us on our mission to make the world a better place through volunteerism.

To get started, simply log in to your account and start browsing our calendar of events. You can also create your own profile and add your interests so we can recommend the best events for you.

We're here to help you make a difference. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.

The Our Good Neighbour Team
